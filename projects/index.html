Updated on December 7, 2022

This Python model monitors Blood Alcohol Content (BAC) levels over time, based on a user’s weight, gender, drink type, and drinking frequency. The data for this project is from Calculus & Mathematica by Bill Davis, William Davis, Horacio Porta, and J. Jerry Uhl. It was published in 1994 by Addison Wesley Longman Inc. I accessed it through A Course in Mathematical Modeling by Douglas D. Mooney and Randall Swift, published by The Mathematical Association of America, 1999.
